Recent Preprints/Publications

  • [2025.06] Our draft Equilibrium Correction Iteration for A Class of Mean-Field Game Inverse Problem is online. In this paper, we propose an iterative algorithm and two variants for reconstruct the ambient potential in mean-field games. The algorithm is different from generic optimization based method and exploits the structure of mean-field games. We also explore the connection between the potential identification problem in mean-field games and in linear parabolic equations.

  • [2024.11] Our draft Convergence Analysis and Acceleration of Fictitious Play for General Mean-Field Games via the Best Response is online. In this paper, we give the convergence rate of fictitious play for non-potential mean-field games by studying the stability of the best response mapping.

  • [2024.10] Our draft Zeroth-order Stochastic Cubic Newton Method with Low-rank Hessian Estimation is online.

  • [2024.08] Our paper A bilevel optimization method for inverse mean-field games is accepted by Inverse Problems.
